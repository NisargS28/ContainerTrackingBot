Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with TraceContainer?

Super simple! Find your container number (usually on your bill of lading or shipping documents), paste it in the search box at the top of the page, and hit "TRACK NOW". We'll automatically detect your shipping carrier and take you straight to their tracking portal with your container number ready to go.

Is my shipping information private and secure?

Absolutely. We never store, collect, or track any of your container numbers or personal data. Your information stays in your browser only. We simply copy your container number and redirect you to the official carrier's tracking page. No accounts, no cookies, no data collection.

Which shipping carriers does TraceContainer support?

We support all global shipping carriers including Maersk, MSC, CMA CGM, Hapag-Lloyd, Evergreen, COSCO, ONE, ZIM, and many more. Just scroll down to see our full carrier directory. Can't find your carrier? Let us know and we'll add it!

What if my container number isn't working?

First, double-check your container number format: it should be 3 letters + "U" + 7 numbers (e.g., YMLU1234567). If that's correct and we still can't find the carrier, try our carrier directory below to manually access your shipping line's tracking system. The format with the "U" is the international standard.

Can I use TraceContainer on my phone?

Yes! TraceContainer works perfectly on mobile phones, tablets, and desktops. Just visit our site from any device, enter your container number, and you'll be connected to your carrier's tracking page instantly. No app needed.

How current is the tracking information?

TraceContainer connects you directly to your shipping carrier's official tracking systems. We don't host the data ourselves. You get real-time updates straight from the source. Update frequency depends on each carrier, but most major lines update tracking information multiple times per day as containers move through the supply chain.

Do I need an account or login?

Nope! TraceContainer is completely free and requires zero registration. Just type your container number and go. Some carriers might ask you to log in on their tracking page (especially for detailed shipping documents), but that's handled by them, not us.

Why should I use TraceContainer instead of going directly to the carrier?

Great question! With TraceContainer, you don't need to remember which carrier you're using or visit dozens of different carrier websites. We automatically identify your carrier from your container number and connect you instantly. Save time, save frustration, and track from one unified platform. No ads, no unnecessary redirects, just pure tracking.
